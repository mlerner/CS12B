Birthday.java

This program will ask the user for their birthday, in the following format - MM DD YYYY.
It will then implement methods created in the TeacherDate class. It will also tell you:
What day you were born on, whether it is your birthday or not, whether your birthday 
was on a leap year, and how many days it is till your birthday. Lastly, it tells you
how many days old you are.
